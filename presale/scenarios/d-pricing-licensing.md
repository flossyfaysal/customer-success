# Scenario D: Pricing & Licensing Questions

**When to use:** Customer asks about pricing tiers, what's included, license limits, renewal terms, or which plan is right for them.

## Approach
- Lead with the pricing page link for self-service
- Summarize the relevant tiers clearly
- Address the specific question they asked (what's included, upgrade path, etc.)
- For new customers: mention COMPLETE60OFF for 60% off
- If they're leaning toward Basic, evaluate whether to recommend Plus or Pro

## Plan Decision Guide
- **Basic → Plus**: Customer needs multiple cloud storage destinations simultaneously
- **Basic → Pro**: Customer has WordPress multisite networks
- **Basic → Elite**: Customer has media-heavy sites (WP Media Cleanup included free with Elite)
