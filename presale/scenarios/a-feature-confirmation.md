# Scenario A: Feature Confirmation Questions

**When to use:** Customer asks whether Duplicator Pro supports specific features (scheduled backups, cloud storage integration, one-click restore, multisite support, etc.). They want clear yes/no answers with details.

## Approach
- Restate each question in bold
- Lead with "Yes," "No," or "Partially"
- Explain how it works in 1-3 sentences
- Turn any limitation into a natural cross-sell opportunity
