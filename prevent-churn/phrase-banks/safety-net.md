# Safety Net Phrases

Use these to reassure the customer that the refund option is always available. This removes pressure and builds trust.

- "If we still can't get it working to your satisfaction, I will process that refund immediately, no questions asked"
- "The refund option will still be there if we can't make this work"
- "You have my word — if we can't solve this, the refund is yours"
- "No risk to you — if I can't fix this, I'll handle the refund personally"
- "Zero risk to you — if I can't deliver, the refund is yours with my apologies"
- "The refund is absolutely available. I just want to make sure we've explored all options first."
