# Investment & Opportunity Phrases

Use these when reminding the customer why they originally purchased, and asking for a chance to deliver on that promise.

- "I truly hope you will give us a chance to make this work before proceeding with a refund. You purchased Duplicator to save time, and we want to make sure your investment is worth it by providing first-class service to get this job done."
- "You invested in this tool to save time — let us make sure that investment pays off"
- "I'd love the chance to prove that this was the right decision"
- "Before we move to a refund, would you be open to giving us one more opportunity to deliver?"
- "You purchased this to solve a real problem — let us actually solve it for you"
