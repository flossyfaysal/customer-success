# Empathy Phrases

Use these when opening a response or acknowledging a customer's frustration.

- "I am so sorry to hear that..."
- "I know how frustrating it is when..."
- "I completely understand why you'd feel..."
- "That must have been incredibly disappointing..."
- "I'm truly sorry this hasn't been the experience you deserved..."
- "I can only imagine how stressful this has been..."
- "You have every right to be upset, and I take full responsibility for this experience."
- "Having [X] fail is completely unacceptable, and I'm sorry you've been through this."
