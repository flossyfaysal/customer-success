# Partnership Phrases

Use these to reinforce that you're invested in the customer's success, not just closing a ticket.

- "Your success is our success"
- "Until you have your backup/migration/solution, we are with you"
- "I'm personally invested in making this work for you"
- "We're in this together"
- "I want to be your partner in solving this"
- "I won't stop until your site is working perfectly"
- "I'm taking ownership of this personally"
