# Solution Confidence Phrases

Use these when presenting your offer to help — after empathy, before the bullet-point solutions.

- "I am confident we can solve this for you"
- "I'm sure we can fix this on our end"
- "I would love the opportunity to make this right"
- "Here's what I'd like to offer..."
- "I'm standing by to help"
- "Here's my commitment to you:"
- "Before we move forward with the refund, I'd truly love the opportunity to make this right"
