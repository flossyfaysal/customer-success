# Tone Calibration by Frustration Level

---

## Low Frustration
*Polite request, minor issue*

- **Tone**: Warm, professional, confident
- **Length**: Shorter (6-8 sentences)
- **Solutions**: 2-3 bullet points
- **Language**: "I'd be happy to help" / "Let's get this sorted out"

---

## Medium Frustration
*Clear disappointment, tried multiple times*

- **Tone**: Empathetic, apologetic, reassuring
- **Length**: Standard (10-12 sentences)
- **Solutions**: 3-4 bullet points with details
- **Language**: "I understand how frustrating this must be" / "Your success is our success"

---

## High Frustration
*Angry, multiple failures, threatening to leave*

- **Tone**: Deeply empathetic, humble, urgent
- **Length**: Longer (12-15 sentences)
- **Solutions**: 4+ bullet points with premium offerings
- **Language**: "I am truly sorry" / "I take full responsibility" / "I personally commit to making this right"
- **Extras**: Consider offering more than standard — refund + extended service, personal call, executive escalation

---

## No Frustration / Appreciation Churn
*Customer leaving due to external reason, not dissatisfaction*

- **Tone**: Warm, grateful, gracious
- **Length**: Short-medium (6-10 sentences)
- **Solutions**: Offer alternatives only if genuinely applicable; otherwise grant refund without resistance
- **Language**: "We truly appreciate your loyalty" / "You're always welcome back"
- **Special**: This is often the right moment to request a Trustpilot review as a personal favor
