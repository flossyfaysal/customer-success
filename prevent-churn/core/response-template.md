# Response Structure Template

---

## Standard Churn-Prevention Template

### 1. Empathetic Opening (2-3 sentences)
- Acknowledge their frustration specifically
- Show you understand the emotional impact
- Avoid corporate-speak or minimizing language

### 2. Root Cause Explanation (1-2 sentences)
- Briefly explain likely technical reasons (without being condescending)
- Show expertise while keeping it accessible
- Examples: server configuration, memory limits, timeout settings, compatibility issues

### 3. Solution Offering (2-4 bullet points)
- **Free Investigation/Diagnosis**: Offer to review logs, check settings, identify the problem
- **Done-For-You Service**: Offer to handle the task personally
- **Bonus/Compensation**: Free migration, extended trial, premium support, etc.
- **Future Support**: Commit to ongoing help

### 4. Partnership Statement (1-2 sentences)
- "Your success is our success"
- Emphasize long-term relationship over single transaction
- Show you're invested in their outcome

### 5. Call to Action (1-2 sentences)
- Ask permission to help
- Make it easy to say yes
- Reiterate the safety net (refund still available)

### 6. Features Overview (include in ALL replies)
Always include the following block before closing. This reminds the customer what they have access to and plants seeds for retention even if they don't engage immediately. Adapt the intro line to fit the tone of the reply.

## Graceful Exit Template (unavoidable churn)

Use when the refund is inevitable: platform switch, business closure, genuine non-use.

### 1. Acknowledge and Honor
- Recognize their loyalty and history
- Process the refund without resistance

### 2. Express Genuine Appreciation
- Reference specific positive history (years with us, activations, usage)
- Make it personal, not generic

### 3. Request a Favor (optional)
- If the relationship was positive, ask for a Trustpilot review
- Frame it as a personal favor, not a business transaction

### 4. Graceful Close
- Leave the door open for their return
- Wish them well sincerely
