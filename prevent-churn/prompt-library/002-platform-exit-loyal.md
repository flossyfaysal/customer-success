# Prompt Library Entry 002

**Input:** Long-term customer (6 years), license just renewed today, requesting refund — moving away from WordPress entirely (to another platform like Wix). No dissatisfaction with the product. License was activated across multiple sites.

---

## Conversation Thread

**Customer:**
"The license just literally renews today. I have been a long-time customer and am requesting a refund as we have no use for this plug-in any longer as we are not using WordPress at all. I hope you consider my request."

**Agent approach:**
- Acknowledged 6 years of loyalty specifically
- Noted license was activated across multiple sites (shows genuine experience and positive impact)
- Requested a Trustpilot review as a personal favor before the customer left
- Processed the refund in full, no resistance
- Acknowledged platform constraint (moving to Wix — no upsell or discount offer makes sense)
- Left door open: "you're always welcome back if you return to WordPress"

---

## Approach Pattern

1. **Honor immediately** — no resistance, no retention pitch for a product they genuinely can't use
2. **Acknowledge loyalty with specifics** — "6 years with us" and "activated across multiple sites" — not generic
3. **Process the refund gracefully** — confirm it's done, give timeline
4. **Trustpilot ask as a personal favor** — frame it as a human ask, not a business request
5. **Platform constraint acknowledgment** — no discount or service offer; they've moved to a different ecosystem
6. **Open door close** — "always welcome back if you return to WordPress"

## Why This Approach Works
- Customers who leave due to a platform switch (not dissatisfaction) are goodwill ambassadors if treated with respect
- Specific details (years, activations) make the appreciation feel genuine, not scripted
- Asking for a review at this moment is natural — positive experience + graceful exit = highest chance of a yes
- The open-door close builds long-term brand equity at zero cost

## Key Pattern
Appreciation churn = honor refund + acknowledge loyalty with specifics + Trustpilot review ask + open door
