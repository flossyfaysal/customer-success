# Negative Review Recovery Skill

## Purpose
Turn a negative customer rating into a positive experience by reaching out with empathy, acknowledging the issue, and genuinely asking how we can make things right. The goal is not just to flip the rating — it's to rebuild trust and show the customer we truly care about their experience.
