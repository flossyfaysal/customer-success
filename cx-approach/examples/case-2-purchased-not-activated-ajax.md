# Case 2: Purchased but Never Activated — AJAX Block Unresolved + Proactive Backup Offer

**Context:** Customer had an AJAX block issue preventing backup creation. Troubleshooting steps were sent. Customer went silent — no feedback on the AJAX fix. License was purchased but never activated on any site.

**Pattern tags:** `purchased-not-activated` `unresolved-issue` `proactive-backup-offer` `make-things-right` `no-guilt-nudge` `easy-exit-ramp`

## What Made This Pattern Distinct
- Combined two observations (unresolved issue + unactivated license) into one natural, caring message
- Didn't guilt-trip for silence — framed it as "I want to make sure you're not stuck"
- Went beyond "share access so we can look" — offered to actually create the backup for them
- "Make things right" language took ownership of their unfinished experience
- Framed the proactive backup as protecting their site while they're away or busy
- Easy exit ramp — if the issue is already fixed, just let us know (no pressure either way)

## Reply

Hi [Customer Name],

I hope you're doing well! I'm just checking in to follow up on a couple of things.

I wanted to see if you were able to try the AJAX settings or the troubleshooting guide I sent over. I haven't heard back yet on the "Communication to AJAX is blocked" issue, and I just want to make sure you're not still stuck on that.

I also noticed that you've purchased your license but haven't activated it on any site yet. No rush at all — but I want to make sure nothing is blocking you from getting started.

Here's what I'd like to offer: if you're open to sharing your site access with us, I can log in from our end, check into the AJAX block issue, and create a backup for you directly — so you can relax knowing your site is protected without any extra work on your end, especially while you're busy.

We genuinely want to make things right for you. Allow us a chance to get this sorted, and you'll have working backups and peace of mind.

Of course, if the issue is already resolved on your end, just let me know — that would be great news too!

Standing by whenever you're ready.

Best regards,
