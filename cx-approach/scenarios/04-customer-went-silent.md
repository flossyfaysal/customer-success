# Scenario 4: Customer Went Silent After Initial Help

**When to use:** Customer reached out with an issue, you helped, but they went completely silent. No response, no update. You want to re-engage warmly.

## Template

Hi [Customer Name],

It's been a little while since we last connected, and I just wanted to reach out to see how things are going.

I know life gets busy, and sometimes these things take a back seat — totally understandable. But I want to make sure your [backup / migration / site] isn't stuck waiting for attention.

If the issue resolved itself, that's great news! If not, we're still right here and ready to help. You can share your site access with us and I'll personally take a look to find a solution.

Your success with backups is our success. We want you to have continuous, reliable backups protecting your work — that's the goal that drives everything we do here.

Whenever you're ready, just reply and I'll jump right in.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
