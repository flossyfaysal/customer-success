# Combining with Other Skills

| Scenario | Primary | Secondary |
|---|---|---|
| Follow-up reveals customer is still stuck and frustrated | CX Approach | Prevent Churn (if they mention refund) |
| Follow-up reveals backup/migration issue persists | CX Approach | WMGS Pitch (weave in naturally) |
| Follow-up confirms everything is working | CX Approach | Review Seeker (great time to ask) |
| Follow-up shows they haven't started at all | CX Approach | Prompt Library > Getting Started Nudge |
| Follow-up + upsell opportunity (e.g., they need cloud storage) | CX Approach | Upsell Pitch (mention after help offer) |
| Customer leaving but had good support experience | CX Approach (Graceful Closure) | Review Seeker (woven into the goodbye) |
| Customer leaving with personal life context | CX Approach (Graceful Closure) | Personal celebration leads, business follows |
| Issue resolved, customer is happy and staying | CX Approach (Celebratory Close) | Review Seeker + Upsell Pitch (if natural) |
| Purchased but never activated + unresolved issue | CX Approach (Purchased Not Activated) | WMGS Pitch (if issue is complex) |
| Purchased but never activated + went silent | CX Approach (Purchased Not Activated) | Prevent Churn (if at risk of abandoning) |
