# Phrase Bank: Follow-Up & Engagement

## Re-Engagement Phrases
- "Just checking in to see how things are going"
- "I wanted to follow up and make sure everything is on track"
- "It's been a little while — I just wanted to reach out"
- "I hope you're doing well! Wanted to see where things stand"

## Status Check Phrases
- "Did you have a chance to try those steps?"
- "Were you able to get the backup working?"
- "How did things go on your end?"
- "I'd love to hear how it went"

## No-Feedback Acknowledgment Phrases
- "I haven't heard back yet, and I just want to make sure you're not still stuck"
- "I wanted to check — did those steps help, or are you still seeing the issue?"
- "No worries if you haven't had a chance yet — just want to make sure you're not blocked"
- "If the issue is already resolved, just let me know — that would be great news too"

## Warm Close Phrases
- "Standing by whenever you're ready"
- "Just reply and I'll jump right in"
- "I'm here whenever you need me"
- "Have a great rest of your week — I'll be right here when you're ready"
