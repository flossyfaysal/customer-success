# Phrase Bank: Closure, Celebration & Personal Life

## Personal Life Celebration Phrases
- "First and foremost, congratulations on [milestone]!"
- "That is such wonderful news — I'm so happy for you"
- "I can only imagine how [hectic / exciting / busy] life is for you right now"
- "The last thing you need is extra stress from [a website / technical issues]"
- "More importantly, we wish you [a smooth transition / a speedy recovery / all the best] with [personal context]"

## Graceful Closure Phrases
- "I've gone ahead and processed your refund immediately so you have one less thing on your plate"
- "Your subscription is now fully canceled — no further charges"
- "We'd love to welcome you back whenever you're ready in the future"
- "Take care, and don't hesitate to reach out if you ever need us again"
- "We're rooting for you and your [project / business / family]"

## Review Ask Phrases (During Closure)
- "If you were happy with the support you received, would you be willing to take a minute to share a review?"
- "Your feedback is so important to us"
- "The biggest compliment you can give is telling others about us"
- "I genuinely appreciate you doing so during such a busy time"
