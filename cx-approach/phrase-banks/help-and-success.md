# Phrase Bank: Help Offers & Continuous Success

## Hands-On Help Phrases
- "Share your site access with us and I'll take a direct look"
- "I can jump in and investigate from our end"
- "Let me take this off your plate"
- "I'd love to log in and find the solution for you"
- "Sometimes it's faster for us to just jump in"

## Continuous Success Phrases
- "Our goal is to make sure you have reliable backups running continuously"
- "We want you to succeed with your backups — that's when our goal is met"
- "Your data protection is what drives us"
- "We're here until your backups are running and your data is safe"
- "A working backup isn't the finish line — continuous protection is"

## Proactive "Do It For You" Phrases
- "I can log in from our end and create a backup for you directly"
- "So you can relax knowing your site is protected without any extra work on your end"
- "Let me handle this so your site doesn't create extra work while you're away"
- "Allow us a chance to make things right"
- "We genuinely want to get this sorted for you"

## Low-Pressure Service Reminder Phrases
- "If you'd rather not spend your time on the technical side..."
- "Remember, our White Glove team can handle everything for you"
- "If you'd prefer a hands-off approach, we've got you covered"
- "Just something to keep in mind if troubleshooting isn't your thing"

## Purchased but Not Activated Phrases
- "I noticed you've purchased your license but haven't activated it on any site yet"
- "No rush at all — but I want to make sure nothing is blocking you from getting started"
- "We want to make sure you get the full value of what you've purchased"
- "Your license is ready whenever you are — and I'm here to help you get set up"
