## KPI Scoring Reference

| Score | Description |
|---|---|
| N/A | Not able to start. External factors or postponed by manager. |
| 0 | Significant problems. Critical issues, did not meet expectations. |
| 1 | Room for growth. Needed changes, didn't meet all expectations, not fully completed. |
| 2 | Great job. Met very high expectations. Solid and on point. |
| 3 | Crushed it. Exceeded very high expectations. Above and beyond. |
| 4 | Made the needle move. Impacted or transformed the business measurably. |
